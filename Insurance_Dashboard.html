Dataset

What data is used in this project?

The report is built on Excel datasets covering 10 years of insurance data. Below is a quick breakdown of what's included.

Policies

Policy ID, Type, Start & End Date, Status, Premium Amount — the core of the dataset.

Policyholders

Customer name, age, gender, state, and segment — used for demographic analysis.

Claims & Payouts

Claim ID, date, amount, status, and payout — tracks the liabilities side of the business.

Investments

Investment amounts, maturity dates, and returns — compared against payout obligations.

Sales & Agents

Agent ID, name, region, hierarchy level, and premium sold — for sales performance tracking.

Data Preparation

Power Query handles cleaning, type casting, and shaping. DAX is used for all calculated measures like growth rate, payout ratio, and ROI.
